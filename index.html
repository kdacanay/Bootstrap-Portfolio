About Me

My name is Ken Dacanay, and I am a second-generation Filipino, and the youngest of four boys. My wife Jen and I have just celebrated our 10th anniversary, and, have just welcomed our first child into the world, Liliana Elise.

I never really know what to write when you are asked to describe yourself. I think the first few sentences were okay, but do they really identify who I am? Of course not. I've been to so many schools, attempting to achieve a degree that would maybe help define me and give me my purpose, but I never truly loved any direction I went, I just went for the easiest, common route. I figured if I was good at it, I would like it and eventually love it.

I do identify myself as a musician, maybe an artist, but definitely a songwriter. But a computer coder? A web developer? Ten years ago, if you told me I would be in a computer coding boot camp from Penn University, I would have laughed. I barely know how to use an Ipod let alone my Iphone, and never knew the difference between a Mac and a PC until like, five years ago. But, it's funny how life works. You never know what's around the corner.

I got sick a few years ago and am now on social security benefits, but I also became a newly wide-eyed dad, and now found myself falling in love, for the first time, the direction I am now on: becoming a web developer.
